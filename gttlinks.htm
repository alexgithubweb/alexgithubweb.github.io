These are my 'hardware' links. Do you remember those things made of printed paper?

  • Title: Etudes & documentation. Revue Technique automovile. Renault 5 GT Turbo.
    Publisher: E.T.A.I. 96, rue de Paris. 92100 Boulogne Billancourt. France.
    Tel + 33 1 46 99 24 24.
    A GTT-only book written in french. It starts with a fifteen page length guide about driving and using your car, probably similar to those that Renault dealers would give you with your new car (not realising that the first owner was always going to lose it before selling the car). Then it comes a complete description of the car. Every car component is showed and dismantled, but the explanations are not so detailed. A very good book, available in Spain at Libro Motor, too.
  • Title: Haynes Service and Repair Manual # 1219. Renault 5. Feb 1985 to 1996 (B to N registration) Petrol. A.K.Legg.
    Publisher: Haynes Publishing. Sparkford, Nr Yeovil, Somerset BA22 7JJ, England.
    Written in english. This is a book about the superfive model, in general, but contains most of the GTT particularities too (phase 2 in a supplement at the end of the book). Very didactical and graphical, containing hints and fault finding. Technical enough to work around a lot of problems. It will take for granted that you drive in the left side -I don't-. A must-have.
    I bought it from a british bookseller, and I know it's available (also via mail order) in Spain at Libro Motor (See below for address).
  • Title: Manual de taller y tiempos de reparación. Guía de tasaciones. Renault supercinco. Septiembre 85.
    Publisher: Unknown.
    Written in spanish. Talks about the superfive model in general, and has the phase 1 GTT particularities. It fully covers some parts, but hardly touches some others. Includes serious bodywork repairing and a guide to see how long will any repairing take. I bought it through a second hand magazine ad, there are more updated versions.
  • Title: Super GTI Mag.
    Publisher: Societé d'Édition de Presse Loisirs. BP 18, Lège-Bourg, 33950 Lège-Cap-Ferret.
    Tel + 33 5 56 03 90 90. Fax: + 33 5 56 03 90 99.
    A bi-monthly french magazine about car tuning. There are plenty of GT Turbo fans in France, and they are really concerned about preparation, being a lot of them featured in this magazine.
