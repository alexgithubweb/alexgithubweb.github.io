Technical specifications

The specs

  • Engine: Four cylinder in-line type C1J.
    • Engine block: cast iron.
      • Wet liner type.
      • Capacity: 1397 cc.
      • Bore / stroke: 76.0 / 77.0 mm.
      • Compression ratio: 7.9:1.
    • Pistons: aluminium alloy.
      • 1 Top compression ring (Thickness: 1.75 mm).
      • 1 Second compression ring (Thickness: 2.0 mm).
      • 1 Oil scraper ring (Thickness: 4.0 mm).
      • Piston axis diameter / length: 20 / 60.4 mm.
    • Cylinder head: aluminium alloy.
      • 8 Overhead single spring valves.
      • Spring free length: 46.9 mm.
      • Valve seat angle: 45°.
      • Valve diameters: 34.3 mm inlet ; 28.9 mm exhaust.
    • Camshaft: cast iron.
      • Lateral in-block, chain driven.
      • Four pressure lubricated bearings.
      • Pushrod length: 173.5 mm.
      • Pressure lubricated rocker arm shaft.
      • Valve clearances (cold): 0.20 mm inlet ; 0.25 mm exhaust.
      • Valve timing with valve clearances of 0.30 mm (inlet) / 0.35 mm (exhaust):
        • Inlet opens: 14° BTDC / closes: 66° ABDC
        • Exhaust opens: 54° BBDC / closes: 26° ATDC
    • Crankshaft: graphite cast iron.
      • Five pressure lubricated bearings.
  • Oil system:
    • Gear type oil pump.
    • Pressure at 80 °C:
      • 1.3 bar (18.85 psi) at 750 rpm.
      • 3.2 bar (46.41 psi) at 4000 rpm.
    • Full flow monoblock oil filter (Type PBR A13618, Champion L203, Purolator L17695, PurFlux LS 590...).
    • Oil sump capacity: 3.7 l (+0.5 l oil filter). ELF synthetic 15W50 oil.
    • Aluminium oil radiator (From Open Kadett GSi) (Replacing old oil-water cooler).
  • Fuel and exhaust system:
    • JR Direct Injection Kit, replacing:
      • Thermostatically controlled air filter entry (26°C/32°C).
      • Air filter (Type PBR BC1180, Champion W109...).
    • Water cooled Garret T2 turbo.
      • 680 ± 30 mbar (9.86 ± 0.43 psi) at 3500 rpm.
      • 700 ± 30 mbar (10.15 ± 0.43 psi) at 5500 rpm.
    • Cup type simplified turbo actuator circuit.
    • Boost pressure safety switch: 1.1 bar (15.95 psi).
    • Air-air intercooler (Thermostatically controlled at 43°C/47°C, converted to full-time full-flow , by locking the flap). Strapped, to reinforce it and painted black.
    • 'Super' (leaded 97 oct) fuel capacity: 43 l (+7 l auxiliary tank)
    • Electric fuel pump:
      • Flow: 60 l/h.
      • Pressure: 2.5 bar (36.25 psi).
    • Fuel pressure regulator:
      • Minimum idling pressure: 275 mbar (3.98 psi).
    • Solex 32 DIS 931 sealed carburettor:
      • Main jet: 120
      • Idling jet: 46
      • Accelerator pump jet: 40
      • Idling speed: 650 ± 50 rpm.
      • CO rate: 1.5 ± 0.5.
    • Momo Corse exhaust tailbox.
  • Cooling system:
    • Pressurized with expansion tank.
    • Blue expansion tank safety valve-cap: 1.6 bar (23.2 psi).
    • Thermostat (83°C/93°C changed to 73°C/83°C).
    • Aluminium crossflow radiator.
    • 5.5 l ethylen glycol based antifreeze(30%).
    • Air fan: 100 W.
    • Air fan thermostatic switch (92°C/85°C replaced to 85°C/65°C). With dashboard switch to force it to work when I decide.
    • Antipercolation fan.
    • Antipercolation fan thermostatic switch (95°C/89°C). With added relay and dashboard switch to stop it when I don't want it.
    • Degassing unit.
    • Front bumper openings. Three big ovoidal openings with mesh.
    • Bonnet vents. Sierra Cosworth style.
